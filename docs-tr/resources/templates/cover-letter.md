# Cover Letter Template

[Your Name]  
[City, Province]  
[Email] · [Phone]  
[LinkedIn URL]

[Date]

[Hiring Manager Name]  
[Company Name]

Dear [Hiring Manager],

**Paragraph 1 — Who You Are + What You’re Applying For**  
Introduce yourself (program/year), mention the position, and give one sentence about why you're a good fit.

**Paragraph 2 — Why You’re Qualified**  
Pick 1–2 experiences (job, project, volunteering) and connect them directly to the job description.  
Use impact language and avoid generic phrases.

**Paragraph 3 — Why This Company**  
Write 2–3 sentences showing genuine interest.  
Example ideas: mission, product, industry, career direction.

**Paragraph 4 — Closing**  
Thank them for their time, express excitement to discuss further, and mention that your resume is attached.

Sincerely,  
[Your Name]
