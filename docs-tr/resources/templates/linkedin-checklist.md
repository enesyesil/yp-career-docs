# LinkedIn Profile Checklist

## Profile Basics
- Professional photo  
- Clean headline (role + direction)  
- Updated city (Toronto / GTA recommended)  
- “Open to Work” enabled (optional)  

## About Section
Write 4–6 sentences covering:
- Who you are  
- Your interests  
- Tools/skills  
- What you’re working toward  

## Experience Section
- Add job, volunteering, or club roles  
- Use bullet points with impact  
- Add relevant projects  

## Education Section
- School  
- Program  
- Clubs or awards  

## Skills Section
Add 10–15 relevant skills:
- Technical  
- Tools  
- Soft skills  

## Networking Routine
Do these weekly:
- Connect with classmates  
- Follow instructors  
- React/comment on posts  
- Message professionals from events  
- Post small wins or project updates
