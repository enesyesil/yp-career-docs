# Templates

This folder contains all the editable templates used by mentees and caseworkers.  
Everything here is designed to be simple, clean, and practical — no clutter, no overthinking.

Where possible, templates are kept in Markdown or LaTeX so they are universal and easy to edit.

---

## 1. Resume Templates

### **LaTeX Resume (Jake’s Template)**
A clean, ATS-friendly LaTeX resume used by many students in tech, business, and science.

**File:** `./jake-latex-resume.tex`  
*(Optimized for Overleaf or local LaTeX compilers.)*

---

### **Simple Resume (Markdown)**
A basic resume that mentees can edit quickly without technical skills.

**File:** `./simple-resume.md`
