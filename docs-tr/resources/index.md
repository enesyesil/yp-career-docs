# Resources

This section collects the most useful tools, guides, templates, and references for both **mentees** and **caseworkers**.  
The goal is to make finding information **simple**, **organized**, and **actionable** — no long hunting, no confusion.

Resources here include:
- Resume templates (including LaTeX)
- Step-by-step guides
- External links (learning tools, job boards, planning tools)
- Caseworker operational resources
- Forms and templates used across the Career Academy

You can browse by category below.
